Narcos







            Narcos is an American crime drama television series created and produced by
            Chris Brancato, Carlo Bernard, and Doug Miro. Set and filmed in Colombia, 
            seasons 1 and 2 are based on the story of drug kingpin Pablo Escobar, who
            became a billionaire through the production and distribution of cocaine.
            Starring            Pedro Pascal, Wagner Moura, Boyd Holbrook
            Genres	        Crime, Drama
            Audio languages	English, Spanish
