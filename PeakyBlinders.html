Peaky Blinders







            Peaky Blinders is a gangster family of Irish and Roma origin epic set
            in Birmingham, England, in 1919, several months after the end of the
            First World War in November 1918. The story centres on the Peaky Blinders
            gang and their ambitious and highly cunning boss Tommy Shelby (Cillian Murphy).
            Starring            Cillian Murphy, Paul Anderson, Helen McCrory
            Genres	        Crime, Historical Fiction
            Audio languages	English
